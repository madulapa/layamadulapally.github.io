In 2019 I co-founded Rally with in Sigma Eta Pi's startup accelerator program. Rally. is a social media platform that allows users to be directly connected with the social issues that they care about. With just a touch of a button, you can see some of the most pressing social issues occurring in the present and instantly find ways to contribute, whether it be through donations or volunteering. Rally. is also a safe, digital community where you can find like-minded individuals who share similar interests in social causes. Now, you can not only share social issues, but you can act as well.

I configured authentication and authorization using Keycloak server in order to create security levels in the backend application while writing documentation on the different functionalities that Keycloak provides for the company's future use.

In January 2020 I competed in the UCSB Womxn Hacks hackathon where I built a web application that aimed to pair younger students who lacked a strong female mentor in their life to a nearby college student.
